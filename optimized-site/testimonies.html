Broadcast Testimony

Jesus is LORD Radio

"My son was born with the virus and carried it for four years. At one decree of the blood of Jesus, it was deleted from his blood."

Adrian Rodney was born on the 12th of April, 2009, into the shadow of a terrible inheritance. His father had passed away from HIV, and his mother, Jacqueline Wesa, carried the virus herself. From birth his body was covered in relentless rashes that no cream and no drug could heal. In early 2010, testing confirmed what his mother had feared most—her firstborn son was HIV positive. A file was opened, a unique patient number assigned, and the young boy was placed on ARVs.

For years she endured the stigma, the rejection, and the unanswerable questions of a mother watching her child suffer. Then, in 2013, she heard of the Ministry of Repentance and Holiness and brought Adrian to the super glorious meeting of the LORD at Uhuru Park, Nairobi. There, at the command of the mightiest prophet of the LORD, the LORD intervened. The virus was removed from his blood.

Subsequent tests confirmed Adrian to be HIV negative. Today he is a healthy young man in high school—living testimony of the LORD's mercy upon his life.

Broadcast Testimony

Jesus is LORD Radio

"I lived with the virus for twenty-five years. When the servants of the LORD declared 'It is well,' heaven went into my blood."

Rebecca Mosi carried HIV for twenty-five long years, faithfully taking her antiretroviral drugs while the world offered no cure. Then a prayer request was sent on her behalf to the servants of the LORD. The mega prophet of the LORD God replied with three words: "It is well." From that moment, the course of her life began to change.

What followed was a journey through the strictest medical verification. Her blood was tested to the highest level—DNA PCR—first at Lancet Laboratory, then again at the Kenya Reference Laboratory. Both returned the same impossible result: HIV negative. The county HIV coordinator declared her file inactive, and the laboratory in charge told her plainly, "God has healed you. Go and take good care of yourself."

Two years on, Rebecca remains free of the drugs and free of the virus. In a single month after stopping her medication, her weight rose from 58 to 68 kilograms. She now lives every day as a witness to the goodness of the LORD.
