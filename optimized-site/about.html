About Jesus Is LORD Radio

Our Mission

Jesus is LORD Radio is dedicated to preparing the way for the return of the Messiah through the preaching of repentance and holiness.

Broadcasting to the Nations

We broadcast 24/7 on 105.3FM and 105.9FM from Nakuru, Kenya, reaching listeners across East Africa and around the world via our online stream. Our mission is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and call people to repentance before His imminent return.
