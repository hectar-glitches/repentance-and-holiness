Repentance & Holiness

Preparing The Way For The Return of The Messiah

Ministry of Repentance and Holiness

Prepare the way for the LORD's return through true repentance and holy living.

Isaiah 40:3

"A voice of one calling: 'In the wilderness prepare the way for the LORD; make straight in the desert a highway for our God.'"

The Call to Repentance

The message of repentance is the foundation of the Gospel. Jesus Christ Himself began His ministry proclaiming, "Repent, for the kingdom of heaven has come near" (Matthew 4:17). In these last days, the LORD is calling His people to turn away from sin and walk in righteousness.

Living in Holiness

Holiness is not optional for the believer. The Word of God declares, "Be holy, because I am holy" (1 Peter 1:16). As we await the return of our LORD and Savior Jesus Christ, we must sanctify ourselves and live lives that are pleasing to Him.

Hebrews 12:14

"Make every effort to live in peace with everyone and to be holy; without holiness no one will see the Lord."

The Coming of the Messiah

The signs of the times point to the imminent return of Jesus Christ. The LORD has shown His prophets that the day is at hand. We must prepare our hearts, turn from wickedness, and walk in the fear of the LORD.

Requirements for Holiness:

  • Complete surrender to Jesus Christ
  • Repentance from all known sin
  • Separation from worldly practices
  • Daily prayer and fasting
  • Reading and obeying God's Word
  • Living by the power of the Holy Spirit
  • Walking in love and forgiveness
  • Witnessing to the lost

⚠️ Warning

The LORD is coming soon! This is not a time for complacency or lukewarmness. The door of grace will soon close. Today is the day of salvation. Repent and prepare to meet your God!

Revival and Miracles

Through the ministry of repentance and holiness, the LORD has performed countless miracles: the blind see, the lame walk, the deaf hear, and the dead are raised. These signs follow those who believe and walk in obedience to His Word.

Make Your Decision Today

Will you answer the call? Will you repent and turn to Jesus Christ with all your heart? The time is short. Choose life. Choose holiness. Choose Jesus!
